A little about me

I am an Interaction Design Student at Ulster University, Belfast. I would love to be able to tell you that I’ve figured out exactly what I want to do at the end of my degree but like most first years I really don’t have a clue.

At this point, I assume you are actually interested in finding out about me. Why else would you be still be here? Therefore, I’ll tell you more about me. When I'm talking to people about my course they often ask me ‘Do you not have to be artistic?’. No, by all means I am not artistic and at school I didn’t study art or any subjects that really were related to what I am doing now. It was only when I did my work experience with a design company that it really opened my eyes to the world of design.

The thing is I am creative, which is what I think makes designers unique and what makes me unique. Basically, this website is for me to show off my work and make myself look impressive, so you are more than welcome to check out below.
